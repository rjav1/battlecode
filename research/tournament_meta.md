## 1. Tournament Format Overview

All three sprint tournaments use the same format:
- **8 rounds** total
- **Rounds 1-3:** Swiss/seeding rounds (R1, R2, R3) with large match counts to determine seedings
- **Rounds 4-8:** Single-elimination bracket (Round of 32, Round of 16, Quarterfinals, Semifinals, Grand Final)
- **Match format:** Best-of-5 (first to 3 wins on different maps from the pool) in the bracket stage
- **Map pools:** Sprint 1 used 17 maps; Sprints 2 and 3 used 15 maps

### Swiss Round Match Counts by Sprint
| Round | Sprint 1 | Sprint 2 | Sprint 3 |
|-------|----------|----------|----------|
| R1    | 16       | 75       | 77       |
| R2    | 64       | 64       | 64       |
| R3    | 32       | 32       | 32       |

The jump from 16 R1 matches in Sprint 1 to 75-77 in Sprints 2-3 reflects the massive growth in registered teams (from a smaller initial pool to 572 registered teams).
